Gravel - The Programming Language

What is Gravel?

Compiled

Gravel compiles to LLVM IR.

Fast

Gravel resolves as much as possible at compile time.

Static and strict

Types don't change. They are explicit, even with inference for native types.

Predictable

No surprises, no hidden changes.

Powerful but clean

Gravel combines low level power with clean, high level syntax.
